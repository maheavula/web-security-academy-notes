30-DAY INSTAGRAM REEL COVER PROMPTS  —  @mahe_r.eddits
Single copy-paste prompt per day. Text is rendered by the AI inside the image.

HOW TO USE: Paste one paragraph into a text-strong image tool — best results with
Ideogram, Google Gemini / Imagen, or ChatGPT (DALL.E). Set size to 1080 x 1920 (9:16).
In Midjourney also add:  --ar 9:16 --style raw  (its text is weaker, so prefer the above).
Keep the SAME you: upload a reference photo of yourself and tell the tool to keep that
exact person/face every time. Signature 'egg' days are 1, 8, 15, 22, 30.
